About Us

We are a young family from Madison, Alabama that loves Florida for its warm weather and many attractions. We have a daughter who enjoys going to the parks and visiting the beaches. As frequent travelers to Florida, we thought it would be so nice to have a home away from home. Hence, we started this journey to create Sunshine Getaway.

We are happy to share our cozy getaway with you to create memories with your family as we have with ours.
