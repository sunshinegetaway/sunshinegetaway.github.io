Book Now

We offer three ways to book your stay with us: Direct (form below), Airbnb, and VRBO.  The preferred and lowest cost option is to put in a booking request with us below.  AirBNB and VRBO listing prices may be different, but the total cost once booking fees are considered is higher when booking through these platforms.

Direct Booking Information:
 - Check the calendar to the right for availability and pricing*
     - There is a minimum stay of 4 nights
 - Fill out the booking request form below on the left
 - We will review your booking request and reach out to confirm the details.
 - We will send you an invoice and payment instructions along with important dates
 - To keep your reservation, 50% of the booking amount is due within 24 hours of accepting your reservation.  We currently accept payments through Zelle (offered through many banks), PayPal, and Venmo.  If you need to book with a credit card, please book through Airbnb or VRBO (additional booking fees apply).
 - The second and final payment is due 28 days before check in
 - The damage deposit (less any damages) will be refunded 2-5 days after check out to the original payment method

Cancellation policy:
 - Full refund if cancelled within 30 days of check in
 - 50% refund if cancelled between 14-30 days of check in
 - No refund if cancelled if fewer than 14 days before check in

*Note that prices shown in the calendar do not include the one time cleaning fee of $175, sales tax (12%), or the ​refundable damage deposit of $300
